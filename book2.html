By (author) Frank Herbert

Set on the desert planet Arrakis, Dune is the story of the boy
Paul Atreides, heir to a noble family tasked with ruling an
turning inhospitable world where the only thing of value is the “spice”
melange, a drug capable of extending life and enhancing
consciousness. Coveted across the known universe, melange is a
prize worth killing for...

When House Atreides is betrayed, the destruction of Paul’s,
family will set the boy on a journey toward a destiny greater
than he could ever have imagined. And as he evolves into the
mysterious man known as Muad’Dib, he will bring to fruition
humankind’s most ancient and unattainable dream.
